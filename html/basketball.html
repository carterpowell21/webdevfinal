About Basketball

Basketball is an interactive team sport. It consists of two teams of 5 players playing head to head.
The objective is to shoot the basketball into your hoop and prevent the opposing team from scoring in their hoop.

  1. Reasons I love basketball:
    • It brings me a sense of peace as I leave behind any stresses or pressures I am currently against.
    • It is a healthy option for exercising and I can enjoy the sport with my friends.
    • It is very entertaining to watch and play basketball.
  2. Skills developed through basketball:
    • Teamwork
    • Communication
    • Persistence
    • Effort
